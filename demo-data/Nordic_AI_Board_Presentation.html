Nordic Supply Direct

AI Value Realization — Executive Brief

Executive Summary

Nordic Supply Direct has strong operational scale and rich data, but inefficiencies in forecasting, exception handling, and customer service create cost leakage and service risk.

Key Opportunity

Apply AI across planning, logistics, and customer interaction to unlock productivity, improve delivery reliability, and reduce inventory inefficiencies.

↑ Service Level
↓ Inventory Costs
↓ Manual Work
↑ Customer Satisfaction

Key Challenges

Forecasting inefficiency
Demand forecasts are manually adjusted across planners and regions
Fragmented data
ERP, WMS, TMS and carrier systems are not unified
Operational inefficiency
Manual triage of delivery exceptions and customer requests
Customer experience impact
Service teams spend significant time retrieving order status

Prioritized AI Use Cases

Now

Customer Service AI Assistant

Unified order, shipment, and delivery insights across systems

Impact: Reduced handling time, improved first-contact resolution

Now

Delivery Exception AI Assistant

Classifies disruptions and recommends actions

Impact: Faster resolution, improved SLA performance

Next

AI Demand Forecasting

Automated, explainable forecasts with exception alerts

Impact: Reduced stockouts and inventory buffers
